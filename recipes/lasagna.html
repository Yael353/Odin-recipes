Delicious Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lean ground beef
  • 500g minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2dl water
  • 1 egg
  • 700g mozarella cheese, sliced
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 5g teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tabelsoons of sugar
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste

Directions

Step 1

Cook in an oven, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until they become brown. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste and water. Season with sugar, 1 teaspoon salt and pepper. Let it simmer for about 1 1/2 hours, stirr from time to time

Step 2

In a large pot with boiling water. Put the lasagna for 10 minutes. when its done drain the lasagna with cold water. In a mixing bowl, mix cheese and the egg, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

Steg 3

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F

Step 4

Now for the assembeling, Start with the meat sauce, over the meat put the lasagna. put the cheese and repeat.

Step 5

Let it bake in the oven for 45 minutes and rest before serving.
