Tuna Salad

Healthy tuna salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can of water packed tuna
  • 1 large stalk celery
  • 1 tabelspoon minced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons of oliveoil
  • 2 dl walnuts
  • 3 teaspoons of lemon juice

Directions

  1. Open the can of tuna, empty it of all the water and put the tuna in a bowl
  2. Cut and mix the other Ingredients and put it in the bowl with the tuna, finish with the olive oil
  3. Enjoy!
