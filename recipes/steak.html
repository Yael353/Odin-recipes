Jalapeno Steak

This is how you make a really tasty Jalapeno Steak!

Ingredients:

  • 500g Entrecote
  • 4 cloves of peeled garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of pepper
  • 2 tabelsoppns of seasalt
  • 1 dl lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano

Directions

  1. Mix the jalapenos, garlic, pepper, seasalt, lime juice and oregano in a blender.
  2. Place the steak in a resealabe plastic bag and pour in the blended mixture. let it marinade in the refrigerator for 4 hours. Take out the stake 1 hour before cooking
  3. Preheat a pan on high heat with some olive oil
  4. Roast the steak for 2 minutes on both sides

Enjoy!
